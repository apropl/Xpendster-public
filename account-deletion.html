Account Deletion — Xpendster

Last Updated: 2026-06-30

You can delete your Xpendster account and associated data at any time. This page explains how to do it from within the app, and how to request deletion if you no longer have the app installed.

Delete your account in the app

The fastest way to delete your account is directly inside Xpendster:

  1. Open the Xpendster app and sign in.
  2. On the Home screen, tap the profile / account icon in the top bar.
  3. Tap "Delete account" at the bottom of the account dialog.
  4. Enter your password (email/password accounts) or re-authenticate with Google.
  5. Type DELETE (all caps) in the confirmation field.
  6. Tap "Delete account" to confirm. The deletion is immediate.

Request deletion by email

If you no longer have the app installed, or cannot sign in, you can request account deletion by emailing us:

Email: xpendster@gmail.com

Subject: Account deletion request

Please include the email address associated with your Xpendster account. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

What gets deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your Firebase Authentication credentials (login/email)
  • All budget plans you created (including transactions, categories, templates, and debt data)
  • Your user profile and preferences stored in the cloud
  • Your push notification token (FCM)
  • Your membership records in any shared plans

What may remain after deletion

Shared plans: Transactions, comments, and audit log entries you added to plans shared with other users will remain visible to those users. The data is attached to the plan, not your account. If you want to remove specific entries before deleting, please do so inside the app first.

  • Local backups stored on your device are not affected — these are files on your device only and must be deleted manually.
  • Deleted data may persist in Firebase infrastructure backups for a limited period before being permanently purged.
